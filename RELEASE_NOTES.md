# Clinical Portal 2026 Release Notes

## Version

v1.0-content-finalized

## Date

2 July 2026

## Summary

Clinical Portal 2026 is content-finalized and deployment-verified for the
v1.0-content-finalized release. All indexed resources are published, with no
resources remaining in draft status and no resources remaining pending clinician
review.

Production checks were completed for the homepage, index, sitemap, and
robots.txt. Final cleanup pages returned HTTP 200 during verification.

This release records the reviewed and deployed state of the portal. It does not
claim clinical perfection or guideline currency beyond the documented reviewed
dates for each resource.

## Known limitations

- The portal is a static, read-only parent education portal.
- It is not a substitute for medical consultation, diagnosis, or treatment.
- It does not collect patient-specific data.
- Medical content requires periodic review.
- Vaccination and schedule-dependent content requires current guideline checking
  before any future edits.
