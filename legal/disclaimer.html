Medical Disclaimer

Please read this disclaimer before using the educational resources on Clinical Portal 2026.

Important Clinical Portal 2026 provides general educational information only. It does not replace medical consultation, diagnosis, or individualized treatment by a qualified healthcare professional.

1. Educational purpose only

The information on this portal is intended to support parent and caregiver education about common paediatric topics. It is provided for general understanding and should not be used as a substitute for direct clinical assessment, examination, or professional medical advice.

2. No individualized medical advice

The portal does not provide patient-specific diagnosis, treatment plans, prescriptions, emergency advice, or individualized clinical decision-making. A child’s symptoms, history, examination findings, age, weight, and medical background may change what advice is appropriate.

3. No patient-specific data collection in the initial version

No patient-specific medical data is collected or stored in the initial version of this portal. The portal is designed as a public, read-only educational website and is not intended for submission of personal medical information.

4. When to seek medical care

If a child is unwell, worsening, has concerning symptoms, or if there is any doubt about the child’s condition, parents and caregivers should seek advice from a qualified healthcare professional. In a medical emergency, seek urgent in-person care through the appropriate local emergency service or hospital.

5. Clinical accuracy and review

The portal is designed to use medically reviewed resources, references, and last-reviewed dates where applicable. However, medical knowledge and professional guidance can change over time. Content may be updated, revised, or withdrawn as evidence and recommendations evolve.

6. Medication and dosing information

Any future medication-related resource or dosing tool on this portal must be interpreted cautiously and only within its stated limits. Medication decisions require appropriate clinical judgment and must not be based solely on general educational material from this portal.

7. No doctor-patient relationship created

Accessing or using this portal does not create a doctor-patient relationship between the user and Dr. Murali Gopal, or between the user and any healthcare professional associated with the portal.

8. External links and references

The portal may refer to external clinical guidance, professional organisations, or educational resources. External websites are managed by their respective owners, and Clinical Portal 2026 is not responsible for their content, availability, or later changes.

9. Use of the portal

By using this portal, users acknowledge that the information is for general education only and agree that it should not be relied upon as a replacement for direct professional medical care.
