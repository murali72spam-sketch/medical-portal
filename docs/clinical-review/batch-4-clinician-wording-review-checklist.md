# Clinical Portal 2026 — Batch 4 Clinician Wording Review Checklist

## 1. Purpose

This Stage 3 checklist is a documentation-only aid for Dr. Murali's manual wording review of the selected Batch 4 parent-education resources. It is intended to help check whether existing parent-facing wording is clear, cautious, and safely bounded before any later wording-update or finalization work.

This document does not edit medical content, metadata, status, `medical_review_status`, or `last_reviewed`, and it does not mark any resource as reviewed.

## 2. Safety Boundary

- Use this checklist only for wording review.
- Do not add new medical advice during this stage.
- Do not add medication names, doses, dose intervals, maximum doses, treatment algorithms, diagnostic thresholds, or current guideline schedules.
- Do not convert parent education pages into urgent-recognition, emergency, diagnostic, or treatment pages.
- Use "Needs clinician confirmation" wherever wording, clinical meaning, urgency level, reference adequacy, or parent action boundaries are uncertain.
- Keep caution higher for Speech and Language Delay, Screen Time and Digital Wellness, Mosquito Bite Prevention, Developmental Milestones, and Positive Behaviour Guidance because they contain safety-sensitive boundaries.
