## Safety Note

Do not approve any guide that contains unverified dosages, red flags, contraindications, diagnostic criteria, treatment algorithms, exclusion rules, or infection-control advice.

Measles, diphtheria, pertussis, COVID-19, and rickettsial fever/scrub typhus require particular scrutiny of infection-control or public-health wording. Meningitis and pneumonia require careful review of emergency thresholds. UTI requires age-specific review of fever, testing, and treatment-boundary wording.
