## Clinician Review Checklist

Apply these general checks separately to each selected resource:

- [ ] Parent-facing clarity.
- [ ] Non-judgemental language.
- [ ] Safety and emergency wording.
- [ ] Urgent-help wording.
- [ ] Age-specific caution where relevant.
- [ ] No overmedicalisation.
- [ ] No unsupported diagnostic criteria.
- [ ] No treatment algorithm.
- [ ] No medication, supplement dosing, emergency management, or travel prophylaxis advice beyond source-supported parent guidance.
- [ ] References and source age.
- [ ] Disclaimer and non-substitution wording.

- [ ] Febrile Seizures in Children: seizure first aid, emergency thresholds, fever context, recurrence reassurance.

## Safety Note

Do not approve any guide that contains unsupported emergency advice, first-aid advice, seizure management advice, newborn safety advice, sleep-positioning advice, travel vaccine or prophylaxis advice, supplement dosing advice, medication advice, diagnostic criteria, or treatment algorithms.

Batch 13 requires particular caution around urgent escalation thresholds, first-aid steps, seizure red flags, newborn breathing/feeding/temperature warning signs, safe sleep wording, cord infection signs, travel vaccine and bite-prevention wording, dehydration or heat illness red flags, and any supplement advice that could be mistaken for a dosing protocol.
