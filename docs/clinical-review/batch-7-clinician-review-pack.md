## 5. Special Caution Notes

### Common Cold

- Avoid medication or dosing advice.
- Avoid antibiotic advice beyond conservative stewardship wording already present.
- Verify respiratory warning signs, fever boundaries, school attendance wording, hydration/rest advice, disclaimer strength, and references.
- Confirm it remains parent education and does not create a diagnostic or treatment algorithm.

### Middle Ear Infection / Otitis Media

- Avoid antibiotic selection, dosing, treatment timelines presented as guarantees, diagnostic algorithms, and patient-specific escalation rules.
- Verify pain/fever boundaries, ear discharge wording, hearing-related wording, antibiotic stewardship wording, review cues, disclaimer strength, and references.

### Tonsillitis / Sore Throat

- Avoid medicine/dosing advice, testing algorithms, antibiotic-selection advice, diagnostic certainty, and fixed school-exclusion rules.
- Verify airway/swallowing warning signs, hydration guidance, fever boundaries, antibiotic stewardship wording, review cues, disclaimer strength, and references.

### Impetigo

- Avoid antibiotic or steroid selection, application schedules, dosing, treatment algorithms, fixed school-exclusion rules, and overconfident diagnosis from appearance alone.
- Verify spread/hygiene wording, skin red flags, household/school wording, treatment-boundary wording, disclaimer strength, and references.
