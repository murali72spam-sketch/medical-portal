Parent Education Portal

Good paediatric care does not end with a prescription. Parents need clarity, reassurance, and reliable guidance they can revisit at home.

Why this portal exists

This portal is being developed as a trusted library of parent education resources: condition guides, feeding and nutrition information, vaccination resources, safety checklists, and practical home-care instructions.

  • To explain common child health problems in simple, accurate language.
  • To help parents recognise warning signs that require medical review.
  • To support clinic counselling with printable and mobile-friendly resources.
  • To reduce confusion from unreliable online information and social media myths.
This educational portal does not replace a consultation with a qualified doctor. Urgent symptoms, breathing difficulty, dehydration, seizures, altered sensorium, or a very unwell child require immediate medical attention.
